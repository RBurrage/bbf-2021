Boxing

Training for both competitive and non-competitive boxers

Competitive

A fully licensed and fully insured full-time Olympic Boxing Training Center sanctioned through USA Boxing, Inc., we are 100% focused on member safety and fitness. With a focus on building champions, our trainers have over fifty years of combined boxing experience.

Non-Competitive

If you are interested in training without sparring, we will accommodate you. Train as hard as our competitive boxers, learn from the same instructors, and attend the same fundamentals workshops. The true boxing experience is always in the training.

Fitness

Strength & conditioning for athletes and the health conscious

Strength & Conditioning

Learn proper technique and increase strength, stamina, and muscle mass with our on-staff strength coaches (included in membership). Whether you're an athlete preparing for an upcoming season or just looking for a change to the same old lifting and treadmill routine our strength and conditioning program is your answer to extreme health and well-being.

Athlete and Corporate Training

If your sports team needs a competitive change to its usual conditioning routine, or if you are an employer looking for a great team-oriented fitness experience for your employees, this program will give your group a motivational bonding experience working within a team dynamic. When individual ability may not be enough to propel someone through a pushup pyramid, hard work, determination and a great deal of encouragement will be. Call or email for details and rates.

Are you an athlete preparing for an upcoming high school or college sports season? Our trainers will give you a competitive edge, explosion off the line, core power and hand speed with a specialized program designed for your success to get you off the bench and into the game!
