Membership

Boston Boxing & Fitness offers two types of memberships:

  1. Standard: Strength and conditioning combined with cardio and boxing
  2. Classes Only: Individual or in packs

The Standard Membership consists of a structured training environment including strength and conditioning, cardio and boxing.

The Class Membership is for classes only, which can be purchased individually or in packs. Our trainer-led classes include 30 to 90 minutes of jump rope, medicine ball and weight-training, plyometrics and lots of boxing.

Class and Standard Memberships are charged individually and can not be combined into one membership.

No experience is necessary. Beginners are always welcome.

Classes

Group classes are taught by certified USA boxing instructors, are available for all levels. Classes include a stretch designed to increase flexibility and prevent injury. The stretch is followed by a series of circuit training exercises to enhance coordination, strength and agility. Finally, you will put on your gloves for work on the heavy bag.
